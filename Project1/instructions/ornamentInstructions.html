1. Follow instructions on the Safety page.

  2. Materials

    • Any piece of wood will do-- so long as you can fit it on the lathe.
    • String or rawhide or leather or yarn.
  3. Cut wood to desired size.
  4. Follow safety on correct use of tools. Create desired ornament.
  5. When finished, use the sand paper pack. Course to least course, to the point that you can switch on the lathe and rub your finger along the ornament. NOTE: Do not actually do this, as you may burn your finger.
  6. If you wish to add anything, such as a wood burning of a picture or a symbol, do so now.
  7. Next, get the linseed oil and put some on either cheese cloth or a paper towel. Rub the ornament down generously.
  8. Finally, get the wax and rub the ornament down more, until you get a sheen that you like.
  9. Turn off the lathe and pull the ornament off.
  10. Drill a hole through the top of the ornament and loop the string or rawhide or leather or yarn through.
