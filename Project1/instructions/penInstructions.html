1. Follow instructions on the Safety page.
  2. Get pen set. Pull out the tubes.
  3. Scour the outside of the tubes.
  4. Get pen blank.
  5. Put the tubes on the pen blank; make sure there is some space between the tubes and the top and bottom of the pen blank.
  6. Mark with a pencil the top and bottom of the tubes on the pen blank.
  7. Cut the pen blank along the center most lines.
  8. Drill a hole through the center of the pen blank halves.
  9. Put wood glue in the newly cut holes, taking special care as to which one is the longer one.
  10. Put the longer pen tube into the longer pen blank, and the shorter tube in the shorter blank.
  11. Place both halves onto the lathe securely.
  12. Refer to Safety page on how to use lathe tools.
  13. When finished, use the sand paper pack. Course to least course, to the point that you can switch on the lathe and rub your finger along the pen. NOTE: Do not actually do this, as you may burn your finger.
  14. Next, get the lin seedoil and put some on either cheese cloth or a paper towel. Rub the pen down generously.
  15. Finally, get the wax and rub the pen down more, until you get a sheen that you like.
  16. Turn off the lathe and pull the pen off. Using a pen press, put the pen together following the instructions in the kit.
