1. Follow instructions on the Safety page.
  2. Get pizza slicer set and pull out the pizza slicer.
  3. Scour the bottom of the pizza slicer, the little nub underneath where the head of the pizza slicer starts.
  4. You will need a large enough wood block for the handle.
  5. Cut handle to desired length.
  6. Put handle onto lathe
  7. Refer to Safety page on how to use lathe tools.
  8. Create desired shape, taking note of the head of the pizza slicer.
  9. When finished, use the sand paper pack. Course to least course, to the point that you can switch on the lathe and rub your finger along the handle. NOTE: Do not actually do this, as you may burn your finger.
  10. Next, get the linseed oil and put some on either cheese cloth or a paper towel. Rub the handle down generously.
  11. Finally, get the wax and rub the handle down more, until you get a sheen that you like.
  12. Turn off the lathe and pull the handle off. Follow the instructions from the kit to put the pizza slicer on the handle.
